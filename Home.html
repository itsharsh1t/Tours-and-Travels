Popular Places

Hotel du Havre

Looking for a place to stay in Paris? Then look no further than Grand Hotel du Havre, a hotel that brings the best of Paris to your doorstep. Grand Hotel du Havre features 24 hour front desk, newspaper, and express check-in and check-out. Plus, guests can enjoy a lounge, which has made this a popular choice among travellers visiting Paris. Close to some of Paris' most popular landmarks, and they are all within walking distance of Grand Hotel du Havre. We’re sure you’ll enjoy your stay at Grand Hotel du Havre as you experience everything Paris has to offer.

Hotel Vacance

Vacance Hotel is an excellent choice for travellers visiting Aguas de Lindoia, offering a charming environment alongside many helpful amenities designed to enhance your stay. For those interested in checking out popular landmarks while visiting Aguas de Lindoia, The rooms offer a flat screen TV, a minibar, and air conditioning, and getting online is possible, as free wifi is available, allowing you to rest and refresh with ease. Vacance Hotel features 24 hour front desk, a concierge, and a rooftop terrace. In addition, as a valued Vacance Hotel guest, you can enjoy a pool and free breakfast that are available on-site. Enjoy your stay in Aguas de Lindoia!
